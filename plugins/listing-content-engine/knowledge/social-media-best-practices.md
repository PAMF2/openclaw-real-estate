## Content Formulas That Convert

### Formula 1: The Problem-Agitate-Solve (PAS)
```
Problem: "Tired of losing bidding wars?"
Agitate: "You've seen 20 houses and lost every offer. Frustrating."
Solve: "My buyers win because I do X, Y, Z. DM me your search criteria."
```
*Best for: Instagram captions, Facebook posts*

### Formula 2: The Hook-Story-Offer (HSO)
```
Hook: "This house sold $40K over asking in 3 days."
Story: "Here's exactly what the seller did before listing..."
Offer: "Want a custom pre-listing plan? Link in bio."
```
*Best for: TikTok scripts, Reels, video content*

### Formula 3: The Before-After-Bridge (BAB)
```
Before: "1,200 sqft starter home, original everything."
After: "$85K in equity after 3 years of smart upgrades."
Bridge: "I help buyers find homes with this kind of upside."
```
*Best for: LinkedIn posts, investor-focused content*

### Formula 4: The Number List
```
"5 things your home inspector won't tell you"
"3 neighborhoods in [City] where prices are still rising"
"7 mistakes first-time buyers make (and how to avoid them)"
```
*Best for: All platforms — high save/share rate*

### Formula 5: The Micro-Story
```
"My client Sarah almost gave up after 6 months of searching.
Then we found [property detail].
She closes Friday.
Never give up on your dream home."
```
*Best for: Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn — high engagement*

### Formula 6: The Stat Drop
```
"The average home in [City] sells in [X] days.
Median price: $[X].
[X]% of homes sell above asking.
What this means for you: [one sentence insight]."
```
*Best for: LinkedIn, X/Twitter, market positioning*

## Platform-Specific Content Rules

### Instagram
- **First line is your hook** — it must stop the scroll
- Use line breaks for readability (no walls of text)
- End with a CTA: "Save this post," "DM me 'HOMES'," "Link in bio"
- Carousel posts get 3x more engagement than single images for listings
- Reels outperform static posts 4:1 in reach

### Facebook
- **Shorter posts (40-80 chars) get 86% more engagement**
- Ask questions to trigger comments
- Native video gets 10x more reach than YouTube links
- Tag the neighborhood/city page for local reach
- Community-focused tone outperforms salesy tone

### LinkedIn
- **Open with a bold statement or number**
- Write in short paragraphs (1-2 sentences each)
- Use "I" statements — personal stories perform 3x better
- No hashtags in the body — put them at the bottom
- Investment angle resonates strongest on this platform

### X (Twitter)
- **Tweet 1 = the hook** (must be standalone and compelling)
- Threads of 4-7 tweets perform best for listings
- Use numbers and specifics: "$525K | 4BR | Corner lot | Smart home"
- Quote-tweet local market data with your take
- Pin your best listing tweet

### TikTok
- **Hook in first 3 seconds** — "Wait until you see this kitchen" or "POV: you just walked into your dream home"
- 15-30 second videos outperform longer ones for listings
- Use trending sounds when possible
- Text overlays are essential (many watch without sound)
- End with a question or CTA to drive comments

## Content Calendar Framework

### Weekly Posting Schedule for Real Estate Agents

| Day | Platform | Content Type |
|-----|----------|-------------|
| **Monday** | Instagram + Facebook | Market update or motivational quote with market stat |
| **Tuesday** | LinkedIn + X | Educational tip or market insight |
| **Wednesday** | Instagram (Reel/Carousel) | Listing showcase or "did you know" |
| **Thursday** | All platforms | Just Listed or Coming Soon |
| **Friday** | Instagram + TikTok | Behind-the-scenes or closing day |
| **Saturday** | Instagram Story + Facebook | Open house promotion (morning) + live coverage |
| **Sunday** | Skip or light story | Rest day or soft neighborhood content |

### Monthly Content Mix (20 posts/month)
- 6 listing-related (just listed, price reduced, open house, just sold)
- 4 educational (tips, market stats, how-to)
- 4 personal/behind-the-scenes (closing day, client story, day in the life)
- 3 community/neighborhood (local business, event, area highlight)
- 3 engagement (polls, questions, "this or that")

## Photo and Video Guidelines

### Listing Photos for Social Media
- **Lead image:** Best exterior shot with sky visible (golden hour preferred)
- **Interior sequence:** Kitchen > living room > master bedroom > bathroom > outdoor space
- **Always include:** At least one lifestyle shot (set dining table, fire in fireplace)
- **Aspect ratios:** 4:5 for Instagram feed, 9:16 for Stories/Reels/TikTok, 16:9 for YouTube

### Video Walkthroughs
- Start outside with address visible
- Move through the home naturally (don't jump between rooms)
- Pause on standout features (3-5 seconds minimum)
- End in the best room or outdoor space
- Total duration: 15-30 sec for TikTok/Reels, 60-90 sec for YouTube/Facebook
