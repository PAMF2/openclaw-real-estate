## UPSIDE POTENTIAL
- Rents at $1,100 may be below market — check comps for $1,200-$1,250 potential (+$400-$600/mo)
- Add washer/dryer hookups ($3K/unit) to justify $50-$75/mo rent increase
- New roof adds to property value and reduces insurance costs
- If self-managing, you keep the $414/mo management fee = $866/mo total cash flow

## RED FLAGS TO INVESTIGATE
- 1985 build: get a full inspection focused on plumbing (galvanized pipes?), electrical panel, foundation
- Check for lead paint (pre-1978 buildings require disclosure, this is post-78 so likely clear)
- Verify actual rental income with 12 months of bank statements from seller
- Check local rent control ordinances for 4-unit properties
- Review tenant leases — are they month-to-month or locked in?
- Confirm property tax won't be reassessed on sale (could jump to $5,000-$6,000)
