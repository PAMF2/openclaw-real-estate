# Condition Letter Templates
> Ready-to-use templates for prior-to-docs and prior-to-funding conditions. Customize loan details, borrower name, and specific conditions before sending.

---

## Condition Bank by Category

Use these pre-written conditions by copying and customizing for each file.

---

### INCOME CONDITIONS

**Most Recent Paystub**
Most recent 30-day paystub for [BORROWER NAME] covering the period ending [DATE]. Paystub must show year-to-date earnings and employer name consistent with verification of employment.

**W-2 Forms**
W-2 forms for [BORROWER NAME] for tax years [YEAR] and [YEAR] from [EMPLOYER NAME]. All pages required.

**Federal Tax Returns — W-2 Employee**
Signed federal income tax returns (1040) including all schedules and attachments for tax years [YEAR] and [YEAR]. IRS transcripts acceptable in lieu of borrower copies.

**Federal Tax Returns — Self-Employed**
Signed federal personal income tax returns (1040 with all schedules) and business tax returns ([SCHEDULE C / FORM 1065 / FORM 1120S]) for tax years [YEAR] and [YEAR]. All K-1 forms must be included. IRS 4506-C transcripts required.

**Year-to-Date Profit and Loss Statement**
Current year-to-date profit and loss statement prepared by a licensed CPA or [BORROWER NAME] and dated within 60 days of the closing date. Statement must be signed and include monthly detail.

**Social Security Award Letter**
Current Social Security award letter for [BORROWER NAME] dated within 365 days confirming current monthly benefit amount. Contact SSA at 1-800-772-1213 to request an updated letter if needed.

**Pension / Retirement Award Letter**
Award letter or current statement from [PENSION SOURCE] confirming monthly payment amount and continuance for [BORROWER NAME].

**Rental Income — Lease Agreement**
Executed lease agreement(s) for [RENTAL PROPERTY ADDRESS] covering the current tenancy period, signed by both landlord and tenant(s).

**Rental Income — Schedule E Verification**
Schedule E from federal tax returns for [YEAR] and [YEAR] showing rental income and expenses for [PROPERTY ADDRESS]. Previously requested with tax returns — confirm all pages are included.

**Employment Gap Explanation**
Written explanation signed by [BORROWER NAME] addressing the gap in employment from [DATE] to [DATE]. If caused by illness, layoff, or education, supporting documentation (e.g., termination letter, enrollment records) must accompany the explanation.

**Verbal Verification of Employment**
Verbal verification of employment for [BORROWER NAME] must be completed within 10 business days of the anticipated closing date. Processing team to complete and document in the loan file.

**Written Verification of Employment**
Written verification of employment from [EMPLOYER NAME] confirming [BORROWER NAME]'s position, start date, employment status (full-time/part-time), and [base salary / hourly rate].

---

### ASSET CONDITIONS

**Bank Statements — Most Recent 2 Months**
Most recent 2 months of bank statements for all accounts used to qualify, including [BANK NAME] account ending [XXXX]. All pages required. Statements must show borrower name, account number, and full transaction history.

**Large Deposit Explanation and Documentation**
Statement(s) show a deposit of $[AMOUNT] on or around [DATE] that is not consistent with regular payroll or recurring transfers. Provide a written explanation and supporting documentation (e.g., check copy, sale receipt, gift letter) confirming the source of funds.

**Gift Letter**
Gift letter from [DONOR NAME] ([RELATIONSHIP TO BORROWER]) confirming that the gift of $[AMOUNT] is not a loan and no repayment is expected. Must include donor's address, phone number, and signed certification. Wire confirmation or canceled check showing transfer of gift funds also required.

**Retirement Account Statement**
Most recent quarterly statement for [ACCOUNT TYPE, e.g., 401k] account with [INSTITUTION NAME], account number ending [XXXX], reflecting vested balance.

**Proceeds from Sale of Prior Property**
Settlement statement (HUD-1 / ALTA) from the sale of [PRIOR PROPERTY ADDRESS] confirming net proceeds of $[AMOUNT] received by [BORROWER NAME].

**Earnest Money Deposit Verification**
Provide canceled check or wire confirmation showing earnest money deposit of $[AMOUNT] paid to [ESCROW/TITLE COMPANY] on [DATE].

**Business Funds — Access Verification**
[BORROWER NAME] lists business account at [BANK NAME] as a reserve source. Provide evidence that [BORROWER NAME] has unilateral access to business funds and that withdrawal will not harm the business. CPA letter confirming adequate business liquidity recommended.

---

### PROPERTY CONDITIONS

**Appraisal — Value Confirmation**
Appraisal must confirm a minimum value of $[AMOUNT] to support the approved LTV of [X]%. Current appraisal on file is pending review / shows value of $[AMOUNT].

**Appraisal — Repairs Required**
Appraiser has noted the following repairs as conditions of value: [LIST REQUIRED REPAIRS]. Evidence that repairs have been completed (receipts, contractor invoice, and re-inspection or re-certification from appraiser) required prior to docs.

**Hazard Insurance Binder**
Evidence of hazard insurance coverage for the subject property at [ADDRESS], with coverage of at least $[AMOUNT] (replacement cost or loan amount, whichever is greater). Policy must name [LENDER NAME] as mortgagee with standard mortgage clause language.

**Flood Insurance**
Subject property is located in FEMA Flood Zone [X]. Flood insurance policy with minimum coverage of $[AMOUNT] (or maximum available under NFIP) is required. Policy must name [LENDER NAME] as mortgagee.

**HOA — Evidence of Insurance (Condo)**
Master insurance certificate for [CONDO PROJECT NAME] reflecting hazard, liability, and fidelity coverage meeting secondary market requirements. Current HOA questionnaire and budget also required.

**HOA Questionnaire**
Completed and signed HOA questionnaire for [PROJECT NAME] reflecting owner-occupancy ratio, pending litigation status, budget reserves, and delinquency rate. Questionnaire must be dated within 90 days.

**Well and Septic Certification**
Property utilizes a private well and/or septic system. Provide certification from a licensed inspector confirming well water quality meets local health standards and septic system is in good working condition. Tests must be dated within 90 days.

**Survey**
Current survey of the subject property is required for [STATE] closings. Survey must reflect current improvements and be signed and sealed by a licensed surveyor.

---

### CREDIT CONDITIONS

**Letter of Explanation — Derogatory Credit**
Written explanation from [BORROWER NAME] addressing the following derogatory credit items:
- [Item 1, e.g., 30-day late on Chase Visa in July 2023]
- [Item 2, e.g., Collection account with ABC Medical, $842]
Explanation must describe the circumstances, how the situation was resolved, and that similar issues are not expected to recur.

**Payoff of Collection Account**
Collection account with [CREDITOR NAME] in the amount of $[AMOUNT] must be paid in full prior to closing. Provide payoff letter from creditor and confirmation of payment (canceled check, wire, or zero-balance statement).

**Judgment Payoff**
Recorded judgment in favor of [PLAINTIFF] for $[AMOUNT], filed [DATE], must be satisfied prior to or at closing. Provide payoff letter and evidence of satisfaction (recorded release or court documentation).

**Bankruptcy Discharge Documentation**
Chapter [7/13] bankruptcy discharge papers for case number [XXXX] filed in [COURT], dated [DATE]. Full filing including schedules required.

**Student Loan Payment Documentation**
Current monthly payment documentation for student loan(s) with [SERVICER NAME]. If enrolled in income-driven repayment, provide current income-based repayment statement confirming monthly payment amount. Zero-payment IBR plans require [0.5% of balance (FHA) / per AUS findings (conventional)] to be used as qualifying payment.

**Authorized User Account Removal**
Credit report reflects authorized user account(s) on [CREDITOR] account. Confirm that [BORROWER NAME] is not relying on this tradeline for qualifying credit history. If credit score is dependent on this tradeline, provide documentation of independent credit history or apply for removal and provide updated credit report.

**Credit Report Refresh**
Credit report in file is dated [DATE] and exceeds the [X]-day validity window. Refresh required prior to final approval.

---

### TITLE AND LEGAL CONDITIONS

**Title Commitment / Preliminary Report**
Current title commitment or preliminary title report from [TITLE COMPANY NAME] reflecting the subject property at [ADDRESS], with no unresolved liens, encumbrances, or title exceptions that would affect the lender's first-lien position.

**Title — Satisfaction of Existing Lien**
Title reflects an existing [MORTGAGE / HELOC / LIEN] held by [LIENHOLDER NAME] in the amount of $[AMOUNT]. Payoff statement and confirmation that lien will be satisfied at or before closing is required.

**Trust Certification**
Borrower(s) hold title in the name of [TRUST NAME]. Provide a copy of the trust certification (or certificate of trust) confirming the trust's existence, trustee authority to encumber the property, and that the trust is revocable.

**Power of Attorney**
[BORROWER NAME] will be signing loan documents via power of attorney. Provide a copy of the executed POA for lender review and approval prior to closing. POA must be specific to the transaction or durable, and must be properly notarized and recorded if required by state law.

**Divorce Decree / Quitclaim Deed**
Property was previously owned jointly with [PARTY NAME]. Provide a copy of the fully executed divorce decree confirming disposition of the property. If [BORROWER NAME] is the sole owner, also provide a recorded quitclaim deed removing [OTHER PARTY]'s interest from title.

**Non-Purchasing Spouse Signatures**
Property is located in a community property state ([STATE]). Non-purchasing spouse [NAME] must sign the deed of trust / mortgage to relinquish any community property interest. Coordinate with escrow/title on required signature forms.

---

### INSURANCE / REGULATORY CONDITIONS

**Flood Zone Determination**
Flood zone certification required for subject property. If property is determined to be in a Special Flood Hazard Area (SFHA), flood insurance as described above is required.

**4506-C / IRS Transcript**
4506-C form(s) signed by [BORROWER NAME] required for IRS income transcript verification for tax years [YEAR] and [YEAR]. Transcript must match tax returns in file. Unexplained material discrepancies will require additional documentation.

**CAIVRS Clearance (FHA/VA/USDA)**
[BORROWER NAME] must be cleared through the Credit Alert Verification Reporting System (CAIVRS). Confirm clearance in the loan origination system before final approval.

**VA Certificate of Eligibility**
VA Certificate of Eligibility for [BORROWER NAME] confirming available entitlement of $[AMOUNT] is required. Obtain via WebLGY or borrower-provided original. If prior VA loan is outstanding, confirm status and remaining entitlement.

**FHA Case Number Assignment**
FHA case number must be assigned and confirmed in FHA Connection before loan documents can be prepared. Confirm case number [XXXX-XXXXXX] is assigned and unexpired.

**Closing Disclosure — 3-Business-Day Review**
Note for closing team: Closing Disclosure must be delivered to borrower(s) no later than 3 business days before consummation. Confirm CD delivery date and acknowledge receipt before scheduling closing.

## Prior-to-Funding (PTF) Condition Bank

**Final VOE — Day-of-Funding**
Verbal verification of employment for [BORROWER NAME] must be completed on or before the day of funding. Confirm active employment with [EMPLOYER NAME] and document call log with date, time, name of contact, and confirmation of employment status.

**Hazard Insurance Paid in Full**
Evidence that hazard insurance premium has been paid for the policy period in effect at closing. Provide paid receipt or confirmation from insurance carrier.

**Flood Insurance — Paid in Full**
Evidence that flood insurance premium has been paid for the policy period in effect at closing.

**Final Inspection (Repair Completion)**
Re-inspection report or appraiser certification confirming all previously required repairs at [PROPERTY ADDRESS] have been completed satisfactorily.

**Payoff Statement — Good Through Funding Date**
Updated payoff statement for [EXISTING LOAN / LIEN] from [LIENHOLDER], good through the anticipated funding date of [DATE]. Payoff must account for per-diem interest.

**Evidence of Satisfaction — Collection / Judgment**
Confirmation that collection account with [CREDITOR] / judgment in favor of [PLAINTIFF] has been paid in full. Provide zero-balance letter, canceled check, or recorded satisfaction of judgment.

**Signed Final Closing Disclosure**
Executed final Closing Disclosure signed by all borrowers required in the closing package prior to funding authorization.

**Wire Instructions Confirmed**
Confirm wire instructions for [TITLE/ESCROW COMPANY] have been independently verified by phone with a known contact at the title company. Do not rely solely on email wire instructions — confirm verbally to prevent wire fraud.

**Occupancy Certification (Investment / Second Home)**
For loans secured by second homes or investment properties: borrower certification of occupancy or investment intent must be in the closing package per applicable product guidelines.
