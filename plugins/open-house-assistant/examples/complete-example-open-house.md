## QUALIFYING QUESTIONS (Print This -- Bring to Event)

### Tier 1: Ask Everyone (first 60 seconds)

| Question | What You're Learning |
|----------|---------------------|
| "Welcome! What brought you out today?" | Motivation level and type |
| "Do you live in the area?" | Neighbor vs. buyer |
| "Are you currently looking to buy?" | Active vs. passive |

### Tier 2: Ask Active Buyers (next 2-3 minutes)

| Question | What You're Learning |
|----------|---------------------|
| "How long have you been looking?" | Urgency and frustration level |
| "What's on your must-have list?" | Whether this property fits |
| "Have you been pre-approved?" | Financial readiness |
| "Are you working with an agent?" | Opportunity to represent |
| "What's your timeline?" | How fast they need to move |
| "What else have you seen that you liked?" | Competition and comparison set |

### Tier 3: Closing Questions (when they seem interested)

| Question | What You're Learning |
|----------|---------------------|
| "What do you think of the price?" | Price perception |
| "Could you see yourself living here?" | Emotional connection |
| "Would you like to schedule a second showing?" | Commitment level |
| "I could have my lender run quick numbers for you -- interested?" | Financial seriousness |

## POST-EVENT FOLLOW-UP PLAN

### Visitor Classification System

After the open house, classify each visitor into one of 4 types:

| Type | Signals | Follow-Up Priority |
|------|---------|-------------------|
| **Hot Buyer** | Asked about price, comps, or making an offer. Pre-approved. Working timeline under 3 months. | SAME DAY |
| **Warm / Not Ready** | Interested but early in search. Not pre-approved. Timeline 3-6+ months. "Just started looking." | Within 24 hours |
| **Neighbor** | Lives nearby. Came to see the house, not buy it. May know someone. | Within 48 hours |
| **Investor** | Asked about rental income, cap rate, or area appreciation. Looking at numbers, not kitchens. | Within 24 hours |
