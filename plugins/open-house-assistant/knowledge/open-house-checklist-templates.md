# Open House Checklist Templates
> Complete checklists for every phase of an open house: pre-event prep, day-of setup, visitor engagement, and post-event follow-up.

## Pre-Event Preparation (3-7 Days Before)

### Marketing Checklist
- [ ] Create and schedule social media posts (Instagram, Facebook, Nextdoor) -- at least 3-5 days before
- [ ] Send email blast to buyer database -- 5 days before
- [ ] Send reminder email -- morning of or day before
- [ ] Post to MLS with open house date/time
- [ ] Submit to Zillow, Realtor.com, and Redfin open house calendars
- [ ] Create and print flyers (50-100 depending on area traffic)
- [ ] Create digital flyer for text/DM sharing
- [ ] Notify neighboring agents and offices
- [ ] Post in local community Facebook groups (if allowed)
- [ ] Ask seller to share on their personal social media

### Staging Checklist
- [ ] Deep clean entire home (professional cleaning recommended for $300K+ listings)
- [ ] Remove personal photos and religious items
- [ ] Declutter all surfaces -- counters, tables, shelves (75% reduction rule)
- [ ] Clear all closets to 50% capacity (buyers open closets)
- [ ] Fresh flowers in entry and kitchen
- [ ] Set dining table with simple place settings
- [ ] Fluff and arrange throw pillows and blankets
- [ ] Ensure all light bulbs work and match color temperature
- [ ] Turn on ALL lights (including closets and under-cabinet)
- [ ] Open blinds and curtains for natural light
- [ ] Set thermostat to 70-72F (comfortable for lingering)
- [ ] Light a subtle candle or use a neutral diffuser (no strong scents)
- [ ] Remove trash cans from visible areas
- [ ] Clean and stage outdoor spaces (patio furniture, swept walkways)
- [ ] Mow lawn and edge walkways (day before or morning of)
- [ ] Put out fresh welcome mat

### Materials Checklist
- [ ] Property flyers / feature sheets (25-50 copies)
- [ ] Sign-in sheet or tablet for digital sign-in
- [ ] Business cards (stack of 50+)
- [ ] Listing presentation booklet (for seller leads)
- [ ] Mortgage partner flyers / rate sheets
- [ ] Neighborhood information sheet (schools, parks, commute times)
- [ ] Comparable sales summary (1-page for serious buyers)
- [ ] Seller's disclosure (copies available)
- [ ] QR code linking to full listing online
- [ ] Feedback forms (optional -- for tracking visitor sentiment)

### Signage Checklist
- [ ] Main open house sign at property (visible from both directions)
- [ ] Directional signs at 2-3 key intersections (check local sign ordinances)
- [ ] Rider signs on main sign: "OPEN TODAY [TIME]"
- [ ] Arrow/balloon cluster at driveway entrance
- [ ] Remove signs within 1 hour after event ends

### Refreshments
- [ ] Bottled water (essential, always)
- [ ] Light snacks: cookies, fruit, or individually wrapped items
- [ ] Napkins and small plates
- [ ] Hand sanitizer at entry
- [ ] Under $400K: water + cookies is perfect
- [ ] $400K-$800K: add fruit platter and lemonade
- [ ] $800K+: consider branded water, charcuterie, sparkling water

### Seller Preparation
- [ ] Confirm seller will be away during open house (sellers should NEVER be present)
- [ ] Request pets be removed or secured off-property
- [ ] Ask seller to secure valuables, medications, and personal documents
- [ ] Confirm alarm code / smart lock access
- [ ] Get permission for photography/video during event
- [ ] Confirm parking arrangements (clear driveway and garage if possible)

## Day-Of Setup (60-90 Minutes Before Start)

### Setup Checklist
- [ ] Arrive 60-90 minutes early
- [ ] Walk through every room -- check staging, lighting, temperature
- [ ] Open all blinds and turn on every light
- [ ] Set thermostat (70-72F)
- [ ] Start any ambient music (low volume, instrumental only)
- [ ] Set out sign-in station at entry (table, tablet/sheet, pens, flyers)
- [ ] Place flyers and business cards on kitchen island/counter
- [ ] Set out refreshments
- [ ] Place directional signs at intersections
- [ ] Place main sign at property
- [ ] Take a quick video walkthrough for social media story ("We're live!")
- [ ] Test Wi-Fi (buyers will ask)
- [ ] Ensure all doors to rooms are open
- [ ] Flush all toilets, run faucets briefly
- [ ] Check that no personal items were left out
- [ ] Park your car away from the property (leave driveway open)
- [ ] Post "We're open!" story on Instagram and Facebook

## Visitor Engagement: Qualifying Question Scripts

### For Every Visitor (First 2 Minutes)

**Opening (warm, not salesy):**
```
"Welcome! Thanks for coming by. I'm [Name] with [Brokerage]. 
Feel free to look around at your own pace. Can I answer any 
questions about the home?"
```

**After they've looked around (2-5 minutes in):**
```
"What are your first impressions? Anything stand out to you?"
```

### Script: Identifying Serious Buyers

```
Q: "What brought you out to see this property today?"
   [Listen for: actively looking, specific criteria, urgency]

Q: "How long have you been looking in the area?"
   [0-3 months = active. 6+ months = may need help refining search]

Q: "Have you seen anything else you liked? What did you think?"
   [Reveals their comparison set and what matters to them]

Q: "Are you working with an agent?"
   [If yes: respect the relationship. If no: opportunity]

Q: "Have you been pre-approved for a mortgage yet?"
   [If no: "I have a great lender who can get you pre-approved 
   in 24 hours -- would you like an introduction?"]

Q: "What's your timeline? When would you ideally like to be 
   in your new home?"
   [Creates urgency and qualifies readiness]
```

### Script: Engaging Neighbors

```
"Do you live in the neighborhood? That's great -- what do you 
enjoy most about living here?"

[Then pivot:]
"I love getting that perspective. Do you know anyone in the 
area who might be thinking about buying or selling? Referrals 
are the best part of my business."

[If they seem interested in the market:]
"If you're curious what your home might be worth in today's 
market, I'd be happy to run a quick analysis for you. No 
pressure at all."
```

### Script: Engaging Investors

```
"Are you looking at this as a potential investment?"

"What does your current portfolio look like -- single family, 
multifamily?"

"Have you looked at the rental comps in this area? The numbers 
here are interesting -- I can pull those for you."

"Are you working with a lender who does investment loans? 
I have some great contacts if you need one."
```

### Script: Handling the "Just Looking" Visitor

```
"No problem at all -- enjoy looking around! The kitchen and 
the backyard are my favorite parts of this home."

[After they browse:]
"Did anything catch your eye? Even if this one isn't the one, 
I can set you up with alerts for similar properties that come 
on the market. What are you looking for?"
```

## Post-Event Checklist (Within 2 Hours After)

### Immediate Tasks
- [ ] Remove all signage (directional and property signs)
- [ ] Pack up materials, leftover refreshments, and trash
- [ ] Walk through property to confirm everything is in order
- [ ] Re-lock doors, set alarm, confirm all lights/HVAC as seller prefers
- [ ] Take photo of sign-in sheet (backup before entering into CRM)
- [ ] Text seller with visitor count and quick sentiment summary
- [ ] Post recap story on social media ("Great turnout today! Still available -- DM me")

### CRM Entry (Same Day -- Within 4 Hours)
- [ ] Enter ALL visitors into CRM with tags:
  - Tag: "open house - [address] - [date]"
  - Tag: visitor type (buyer, neighbor, investor, agent)
  - Tag: temperature (hot, warm, cold)
- [ ] Add notes for each visitor (what they said, their situation, follow-up priority)
- [ ] Create follow-up tasks based on visitor type (see follow-up sequences below)

### Follow-Up Tasks by Visitor Type

**Serious Buyers (Hot) -- Follow up SAME DAY:**
- [ ] Text within 2 hours: Thank you + offer private showing
- [ ] Email within 4 hours: Listing details + comparable properties
- [ ] Call next morning: Check interest level, offer to write an offer
- [ ] Add to 7-day intensive follow-up sequence

**Interested / Not Ready (Warm) -- Follow up within 24 hours:**
- [ ] Email next morning: Thank you + "here's what I noticed about your search"
- [ ] Text in 3 days: New listing that matches their criteria
- [ ] Add to 14-day nurture sequence with value-add content
- [ ] Schedule check-in call for 7 days out

**Neighbors -- Follow up within 48 hours:**
- [ ] Email: "Thanks for stopping by + what your home might be worth"
- [ ] Include 1-page market snapshot for the neighborhood
- [ ] Ask for referrals: "Know anyone thinking of buying or selling?"
- [ ] Add to monthly neighborhood market update email list

**Investors -- Follow up within 24 hours:**
- [ ] Email: Rental comps + cap rate analysis for the property
- [ ] Offer to send deal analysis for other investment opportunities
- [ ] Connect with lender who does DSCR / investment loans
- [ ] Add to investor-focused email list

### Seller Report (Send Within 24 Hours)
- [ ] Total visitor count
- [ ] Breakdown by type (buyers, neighbors, investors, agents)
- [ ] Common feedback themes (positive and negative)
- [ ] Price perception (did visitors think it was priced right?)
- [ ] Comparable activity (what else are visitors looking at?)
- [ ] Recommendation: hold price, adjust, or schedule another open house
