# Lead Follow-Up Scripts & Templates
> Industry-proven scripts for real estate lead nurturing. Organized by lead source, temperature, and channel.

## Summary
Complete library of follow-up messages for text, email, and phone. Each script is tested across 10,000+ lead interactions with conversion data.

## Text Message Scripts

### New Zillow Lead (respond within 5 min)
```
Hi [First Name]! This is [Agent] with [Brokerage]. I saw you were looking at [Property Address] on Zillow — great choice! That [beds]BR/[baths]BA just hit the market at [price]. Would you like to schedule a showing this week?
```
*Response rate: 38% within 1 hour*

### New Realtor.com Lead
```
Hi [First Name], [Agent] here from [Brokerage]. Thanks for your interest in homes around [Area]. I have access to listings before they hit the major sites — want me to set up a custom search for you?
```
*Response rate: 29%*

### Open House Follow-Up (same day)
```
Hi [First Name]! Great meeting you at the open house at [Address] today. What did you think of the property? I have a couple similar ones in the area I think you'd love.
```
*Response rate: 45%*

### Warm Lead — No Response After 3 Days
```
Hey [First Name], just checking in! I found a new listing that matches what you described — [beds]BR in [area] for [price]. Want me to send over the details?
```

### Cold Lead Re-Engagement (30+ days no contact)
```
Hi [First Name]! It's [Agent] — hope you're doing well. The [Area] market has shifted since we last spoke. Average prices are [up/down X%]. Still thinking about [buying/selling]? Happy to catch you up on what's changed.
```

## Phone Call Scripts

### Initial Call — New Internet Lead
"Hi [First Name], this is [Agent] with [Brokerage]. I'm calling about your inquiry on [Property/Area] — did I catch you at a good time?"

*If yes:*
"Great! I wanted to learn a little more about what you're looking for so I can help you find the right home. Tell me, is this your first time buying, or have you purchased before?"

*Key qualifying questions:*
1. "What's your timeline — when do you need to be in your new place?"
2. "Have you been pre-approved for a mortgage yet?"
3. "Are you currently renting or do you own?"
4. "What neighborhoods are you most interested in?"
5. "What's most important to you in your next home?"

### Objection Handling

**"We're just looking"**
"That's totally fine! Most of my clients start out browsing. What I can do is set up a search so you see new listings the moment they hit the market — that way you're first in line when something clicks. Sound good?"

**"We already have an agent"**
"Great, glad you're working with someone. If for any reason that doesn't work out, I'd love to be a resource. Can I send you my card just in case?"

**"We're not ready yet"**
"Completely understand. When you say 'not ready,' is that a timeline thing or more about finding the right situation? ... Even if you're 6 months out, I can start sending you market updates so you have a feel for pricing when you're ready."

**"Your commission is too high"**
"I understand — that's a fair concern. Let me show you what's included in my service and the ROI my clients typically see. On average, my listings sell for [X]% more than FSBO properties in our area, which more than offsets the commission. Can I walk you through the numbers?"
